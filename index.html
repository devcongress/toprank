What is TopRank?

TopRank is a hideout for programmers of different ages and experience level but we’re more suited for beginners who want to get dramatically better at their craft. We don’t promise anything, exactly, and here’s why. The education at TopRank is self-directed.

TopRank (both the program and environment) provides the right balance of freedom and responsibility, support and challenge, that should help you develop yourself and your craft. We encourage you to come in knowing what you want to be good at but, of course, our resident professionals can help you decide.

Self-driven Learning

We encourage hackers to come in knowing what they want to be good at, what they want to learn. While we don’t teach one programming language or the other, we expect hackers to follow along instructional classes with programming languages of their choice.

It’s the same for assignments. Hackers would use any programming language of their choice unless explicitly determined by their instructors. The same is true of other technologies.
